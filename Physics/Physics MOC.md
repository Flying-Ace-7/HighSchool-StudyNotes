### SUVAT Eqns
>[!Query] Legend
> - s : Displacement
> - u : intitial Velocity
> -  v: Final velocity / velocity
> - a : Acceleration
> - t: time

>[!success] 4 SUVAT Equations
>1:
 >$$
v=u+at
 >$$
>2:
>$$
s=t \times \frac{u+v}{2}
>$$
>3:
>$$
s=\frac{1}{2}at^2+ut
>$$
>4:
>$$
v^2=u^2 +2as
>$$
