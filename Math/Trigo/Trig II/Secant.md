## Reciprocal of Cosine
Thats is to say:
	$$
\sec\theta=\frac{1}{\cos\theta}
$$
Hence, in a right-angled triangle:
$$
\sec \theta= \frac{hyp}{adj}
$$
and in a circle:
$$
\sec \theta= \frac{r}{x}
$$
