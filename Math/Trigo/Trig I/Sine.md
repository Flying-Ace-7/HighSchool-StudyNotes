In a right triangle: 
$$
\sin\theta=\frac{hyp}{opp}
$$
Where, hyp. represents hypotenuse, opp represents opposite side

## Area of a triangle

When two sides $a,b$ of any triangle and the included angle $\angle c$ is known, the area is:
$$
\frac{1}{2}ab\sin(\angle c)
$$
